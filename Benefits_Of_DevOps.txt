Benefits of DevOps

Faster Time to Market:

Accelerates the development and delivery of software, allowing for quicker responses to market changes and customer feedback.

Improved Quality:

Automated testing and continuous integration ensure that code is tested thoroughly, reducing the likelihood of bugs.

Increased Efficiency:

Automation of repetitive tasks frees up time for developers and operations to focus on higher-value activities.

Greater Reliability:

Continuous monitoring and logging help detect and resolve issues faster, improving system reliability.

Scalability:

Microservices and IaC allow for scalable and flexible infrastructure management.
